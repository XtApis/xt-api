import CodeBlock from '@theme/CodeBlock';

**类型:** GET

**描述:** `/v4/finance/product-position`

判断用户是否已申购某产品。

### 请求参数

| 名称 | 类型 | 必填 | 描述 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| productId | long | 是 | 产品ID |

### 请求示例

<CodeBlock language="bash" title="Request">
{`curl -G "https://sapi.xt.com/v4/finance/product-position" \\
  -H "validate-appkey: $APPKEY" \\
  -H "validate-timestamp: $TIMESTAMP" \\
  -H "validate-signature: $SIGNATURE" \\
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \\
  -d "productId=1001"
`}
</CodeBlock>

### 响应示例

<CodeBlock language="json" title="Response">
{`{
  "rc": 0,
  "mc": "SUCCESS",
  "ma": [],
  "result": "true"
}`}
</CodeBlock>

### 响应字段

| 字段 | 类型 | 描述 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| result | string | "true"表示已申购，"false"表示未申购 |

---

## 判断是否已申购结构化产品

**描述:**

### 响应示例

  "rc": 0,

  "ma": [],

}`}
</CodeBlock>
