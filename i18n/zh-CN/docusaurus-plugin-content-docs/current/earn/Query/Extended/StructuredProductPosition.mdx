import CodeBlock from '@theme/CodeBlock';

**类型:** GET

**描述:**
- 鲨鱼鳍: `/v4/finance/product-position/shark`
- 趋势智盈: `/v4/finance/product-position/smart`

判断用户是否已申购结构化产品（鲨鱼鳍或趋势智盈）。

### 请求参数

| 名称 | 类型 | 必填 | 描述 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| productId | long | 是 | 产品ID |

### 请求示例 (鲨鱼鳍)

<CodeBlock language="bash" title="Request">
{`curl -G "https://sapi.xt.com/v4/finance/product-position/shark" \\
  -d "productId=1001" \\
  -H "validate-appkey: $APPKEY" \\
  -H "validate-timestamp: $TIMESTAMP" \\
  -H "validate-signature: $SIGNATURE" \\
  -H "Content-Type: application/json"
`}
</CodeBlock>

### 请求示例 (趋势智盈)

<CodeBlock language="bash" title="Request">
{`curl -G "https://sapi.xt.com/v4/finance/product-position/smart" \\
  -d "productId=1001" \\
  -H "validate-appkey: $APPKEY" \\
  -H "validate-timestamp: $TIMESTAMP" \\
  -H "validate-signature: $SIGNATURE" \\
  -H "Content-Type: application/json"
`}
</CodeBlock>

### 响应示例

<CodeBlock language="json" title="Response">
{`{
  "rc": 0,
  "mc": "SUCCESS",
  "ma": [],
  "result": "false"
}`}
</CodeBlock>

### 响应参数

| 名称 | 类型 | 描述 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| result | string | "true" 表示已申购，"false" 表示未申购 |
